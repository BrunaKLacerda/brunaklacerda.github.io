BI Analyst — Rappi Brasil

Turbo, the quick-commerce arm. Nov 2022 – Sep 2024, promoted from intern to analyst within eight months. I analyzed sales and inventory across nine Brazilian markets and ran the weekly Portfolio Review — the forum where the heads of category management and supply, and the GM of Turbo Brasil, made assortment decisions on the products my analysis gated in, flagged, or blocked.

04

PCOS detection with deep learning

A multimodal study comparing classical machine learning against neural networks for detecting polycystic ovary syndrome, across clinical records and ovarian ultrasound images. I trained and tuned logistic regression, tree ensembles, an MLP, a custom CNN, and a HOG+SVM baseline, then compared them on recall and F1 rather than raw accuracy — because in screening, a missed case costs more than a false alarm. The classical pipeline beat the CNN. A proof of concept on academic data, not a clinical tool.
